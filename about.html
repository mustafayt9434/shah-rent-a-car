Our Story

Driving Excellence Since 2010

Shah Rent A Car was founded in Peshawar in 2010 with a simple vision — to deliver world-class car rental experiences across Pakistan. What started with a small fleet has grown into one of the most trusted names in premium transportation.

Today, with 15+ vehicles ranging from economy hatchbacks to luxury SUVs, we serve thousands of satisfied customers — from corporate executives and wedding parties to families exploring Pakistan's breathtaking northern areas.

Our commitment to cleanliness, reliability, and genuine hospitality sets us apart. Every car is meticulously maintained and sanitized. Every driver is verified and experienced. Every journey is crafted with premium care.
